    • UI/UX Project - SpeechIt

    Imagine that you have an important speech that you need to present to an audience.
    Now, think what would happen if you did not practice in front of the mirror, but in front
    of the phone. As part of the final project at Sapir College, I was asked to characterize
    and design an application that will give the user a platform to practice his speech,
    and at the end will provide qualitative and quantitative feedback that will improve
    the funding of standing in front of a stage and presentation.
